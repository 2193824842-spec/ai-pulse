Mistral AI just released Voxtral TTS on March 23, 2026 — a lightweight, open-weights text-to-speech model that is changing how developers build voice agents.

Why Voice Agents Matter Now

Voice is becoming the primary interface for human-computer interaction again. Most existing TTS systems fall into two camps: lightweight open-source models that sound artificial, or expensive proprietary APIs. Voxtral TTS bridges that gap with open weights you can run locally.

Voxtral TTS is built on Mistral Ministral 3B backbone with a 3.4B parameter transformer decoder, 390M flow-matching acoustic transformer, and 300M neural audio codec. Model latency is 70ms for typical inputs.

Multilingual and Emotionally Expressive

Supports 9 languages natively. Achieves superior naturalness compared to ElevenLabs Flash v2.5. Can adapt to a new voice with as little as a 3-second reference sample.
