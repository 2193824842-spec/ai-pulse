Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

AI Insights Blog ("we", "our", "this site") is a static blog hosted on GitHub Pages. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about the limited data we collect.

Data We Collect

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (measurement ID: G-8TZNRYLZ15) to understand how visitors use this site. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website or search engine
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Browser type and device information

This data is aggregated and anonymous. We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information through analytics.

Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique visitors. You can opt out by:

  • Using your browser's "Do Not Track" setting
  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Disabling cookies in your browser settings

Server Logs

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information such as IP addresses in server logs. See GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • We do not use advertising or tracking pixels
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties
  • We do not require account creation or login

Third-Party Services

  • GitHub Pages — site hosting (privacy policy)
  • Google Analytics — traffic analytics (privacy policy)
  • Pexels — stock images used in articles (privacy policy)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
