What is Sora?

Sora is OpenAI's groundbreaking text-to-video model capable of generating realistic and imaginative video scenes from text prompts. It can create videos up to 1 minute long with high visual quality and understanding of physics, motion, and scene continuity. Available to ChatGPT Plus and Pro subscribers.

Core Features

Text-to-video generation (up to 1 minute)
High-resolution video output (up to 1080p)
Image-to-video animation
Video-to-video style transfer
Storyboard mode for multi-scene videos
Remix: modify existing videos with prompts
Loop: create seamlessly looping videos
Blend: merge two videos together
Understanding of physics and real-world motion
Multiple aspect ratios (16:9, 9:16, 1:1)
Integrated into ChatGPT interface
Watermarking via C2PA metadata

Pros & Cons

👍 Pros

  • Most realistic physics and motion understanding of any video AI
  • Integrated into ChatGPT for easy access
  • Supports diverse video styles from cinematic to animated
  • Storyboard mode enables multi-scene storytelling
  • Continuous improvement from OpenAI's research

👎 Cons

  • No free tier — requires ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) minimum
  • Video length limited to 20 seconds on Pro, 5 seconds on Plus
  • Generation can be slow during peak times
  • Limited editing control compared to traditional video tools
  • Watermarked on Plus tier
