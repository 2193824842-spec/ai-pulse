What is CrushOn AI?

CrushOn AI is a platform that allows users to create, customize, and interact with AI-powered characters in unrestricted conversations. The platform emphasizes creative roleplay and character development with minimal content moderation, enabling users to explore diverse conversational scenarios with personalized AI companions.

Core Features

Unrestricted character creation and customization
NSFW-friendly conversations without strict content filters
Diverse pre-made character library
Memory and personality persistence across conversations
Multiple conversation styles and interaction modes
Community-created character sharing
