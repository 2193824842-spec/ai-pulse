What is Canva AI?

Canva is the world's most popular design platform, now supercharged with AI. Magic Studio brings AI image generation, background removal, text-to-image, and AI-powered design suggestions to Canva's drag-and-drop editor. Used by 150M+ people for social media, presentations, marketing materials, and more.

Core Features

Magic Media: text-to-image and text-to-video generation
Magic Eraser: remove objects from photos
Background Remover with one click
Magic Resize: adapt designs to any format
Magic Write: AI copywriting assistant
Magic Animate: auto-animate designs
Brand Kit for consistent brand assets
100M+ stock photos, videos, and elements
Presentation mode with AI speaker notes
Team collaboration in real-time
Print-on-demand integration
Mobile apps for iOS and Android

Pros & Cons

👍 Pros

  • Most accessible design tool — no design skills required
  • Comprehensive AI suite (Magic Studio) for non-designers
  • 150M+ users means massive template and asset library
  • Excellent for social media, presentations, and marketing
  • Affordable Pro plan at $15/mo with full AI access

👎 Cons

  • Not suitable for complex professional design work
  • AI image generation quality lags behind Midjourney
  • Free tier has limited AI features
  • Can feel cluttered with too many options
  • Print quality can be inconsistent
