What is Grammarly?

Grammarly is the world's most widely used AI writing assistant, helping over 30 million people write more clearly and effectively. It checks grammar, spelling, punctuation, clarity, and tone in real-time across browsers, desktop apps, and Microsoft Office. GrammarlyGO adds generative AI for drafting and rewriting.

Core Features

Real-time grammar and spelling correction
Clarity and conciseness suggestions
Tone detection and adjustment
Plagiarism checker (Premium)
GrammarlyGO: generative AI for drafting and rewriting
Browser extension for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
Microsoft Word and Outlook integration
Google Docs integration
Desktop app for Windows and Mac
Mobile keyboard for iOS and Android
Style guide enforcement (Business)
Team analytics and reporting (Business)

Pros & Cons

👍 Pros

  • Works everywhere — browser, Word, Google Docs, mobile
  • Best grammar and clarity checking in the industry
  • Tone detection helps match writing to audience
  • Free tier is genuinely useful for basic corrections
  • GrammarlyGO adds solid generative AI capabilities

👎 Cons

  • Premium plan required for advanced features ($12/mo)
  • Can be overly aggressive with suggestions
  • GrammarlyGO less capable than dedicated AI writing tools
  • Privacy concerns — all text is sent to Grammarly servers
  • Plagiarism checker not as comprehensive as Turnitin
