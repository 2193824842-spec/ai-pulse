What is Beautiful.ai?

Beautiful.ai is an AI-powered presentation tool that automatically formats and designs slides as you add content. Smart Slide templates adapt to your content, ensuring professional-looking presentations without manual design work. Popular with business teams for pitch decks and reports.

Core Features

Smart Slide templates that auto-format content
AI design suggestions as you type
100+ Smart Slide templates
Brand kit for consistent styling
Real-time team collaboration
Presenter mode with speaker notes
Analytics: track viewer engagement
Export to PDF and PowerPoint
Stock photo and icon library
Custom themes and color palettes
Version history
Embed videos and charts
