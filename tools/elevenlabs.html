What is ElevenLabs?

ElevenLabs is the leading AI voice generation platform, offering ultra-realistic text-to-speech, voice cloning, and dubbing in 29+ languages. Used by content creators, publishers, and enterprises for audiobooks, podcasts, videos, and accessibility tools.

Core Features

Ultra-realistic text-to-speech in 29+ languages

Voice library with 1000+ pre-made voices
Instant voice cloning (30 seconds of audio)
Professional voice cloning (high-fidelity)

Projects: long-form audio production
